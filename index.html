Rome is the capital city of Italy and of the Lazio region. It is by far one of the most popular tourist destinations with many popular must see sights.




When to go and how to get there

The best time to go to this artistic world is from mid-March to May from which there isn't a big crowd and warm temperature.

The best way to get there is via airplanes if you live far across the other continents, or there's still the option of going by train but for that of course you need to be close in Europe.

Things to see and do

The best places to see are the following:

  • Colosseum
  • Trevi fountain
  • Pantheon
  • Roman Forum
  • St. Peter's basilica
  • Villa Borghese
  • Spanish Steps
  • Piazza Navona
